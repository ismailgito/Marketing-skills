## Description: <br>
Generates SEO/GEO performance reports, traffic summaries, ranking reports, KPI dashboards, stakeholder updates, and monthly reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Marketing teams, SEO practitioners, and business stakeholders use this skill to turn organic search, GEO/AI visibility, ranking, backlink, content, and ROI data into concise performance reports with prioritized next actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Analytics, search, ranking, or AI visibility inputs may contain sensitive business performance data. <br>
Mitigation: Use read-only, domain-scoped integrations or exports where possible and include only the data needed for the requested report. <br>
Risk: Saved monitoring summaries may persist sensitive findings or unfinished decisions. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated summaries before approving saves to memory and omit unnecessary sensitive details. <br>
Risk: Broad trigger phrases such as generic monthly reports may route unrelated reporting requests into this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the domain, period, audience, report type, and focus areas before producing the report. <br>
