## Description: <br>
Analyzes competitor SEO and GEO strategies across keywords, content, backlinks, AI citations, and traffic share to reveal gaps and opportunities. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aaron-he-zhu](https://clawhub.ai/user/aaron-he-zhu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Marketing, SEO, GEO, and competitive-intelligence practitioners use this skill to compare domains, benchmark competitors, and identify keyword, content, backlink, AI-citation, and positioning gaps. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Competitor research notes may contain private business strategy or market intelligence. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only for user-directed research, review saved notes, and avoid providing confidential credentials or proprietary data unless the agent should use that information. <br>
Risk: Competitor analysis may be incomplete or misleading when tool data, market context, or source dates are missing. <br>
Mitigation: Ask for competitor URLs, user site metrics, industry context, and dated sources before relying on the recommendations. <br>
