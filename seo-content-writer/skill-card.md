## Description: <br>
Drafts SEO-focused articles, blog posts, landing pages, and product copy with keyword planning, structured headings, snippet-oriented sections, and evidence checks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[aaron-he-zhu](https://clawhub.ai/user/aaron-he-zhu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Content marketers, writers, founders, and SEO practitioners use this skill to turn a topic, keyword, or content brief into search-optimized copy with titles, metadata, headings, FAQ sections, link recommendations, and a final SEO review. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Saved memory summaries could retain private content strategy, analytics, or unpublished draft details. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm memory saves with the user and omit confidential strategy, analytics, or unpublished details unless retention is intended. <br>
Risk: Connected SEO tools or Search Console data may expose private performance metrics or business-sensitive keywords. <br>
Mitigation: Use connected data only when the user authorizes it, and review generated claims and citations before publication. <br>
